SDI 1302: Kevin O'Toole

Project 3

Kevin must go to the local store to pick up some groceries. His mother only gave him $25 to pick up the items on the list she gave him. The things he must pick up are: 2 eggs, 1 milk, 1 bread, and 2 waters. If there is any money left over, Kevin can buy a snack! After Kevin gets home, If he completes his chores, he can watch his favorite cartoons and eat his snacks before going to bed!
